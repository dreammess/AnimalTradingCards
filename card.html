Sheep!

A flock of sheep can solve a murder mystery!

  • Binomial Name: Ovis Aries
  • Life Span: 5 to 10 years
  • Diet: Grasses and low-lying plants
  • Habitat: Mountains and grasslands

Sheep were domesticated 10000 years ago in Central Asia. There are 34.2 million sheep in New Zealand, or approximately seven sheep for every human. This, however, is still quite a change from 1982, when New Zealand had 70.3 million sheep, or an astounding 22 sheep for every person!
